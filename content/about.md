## Hi, I'm Po-Wei Huang

I'm a signal processing engineer and software developer with a background in electrical and computer engineering (NCTU / NYCU, Taiwan).

My research focuses on **remote photoplethysmography (rPPG)** — measuring physiological signals such as heart rate and respiratory rate from video using computer vision and signal processing techniques.

---

## Research Interests

- Remote Photoplethysmography (rPPG)
- Biomedical Signal Processing
- Digital Signal Processing (DSP)
- Computer Vision

---

## Highlights

- **3rd Place** — IEEE CVPR / CVPM 2020 Challenge on Remote Physiological Signal Sensing (125 participants)
- Published work on heart rate and respiratory rate estimation via camera-based rPPG
